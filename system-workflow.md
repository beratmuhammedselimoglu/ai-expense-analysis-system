# System Workflow

This document explains how the AI-powered expense analysis system operates.

## Step 1 — Data Input
Financial expense data is provided in structured format (spreadsheet or table).

## Step 2 — Expense Classification
AI interprets the expense descriptions and categorizes them into:
- Operational Costs
- Strategic Investments
- Fixed vs Variable Costs

## Step 3 — Cost Evaluation
The system evaluates:
- Over-spending areas
- Redundant cost centers
- Inefficient allocations

## Step 4 — Insight Generation
AI produces:
- Executive summary
- Risk areas
- Optimization suggestions

## Step 5 — Reporting
Outputs are formatted into a management-friendly financial analysis report.
