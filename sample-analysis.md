# Sample AI Expense Analysis Output

## Executive Summary
The company's Q2 expense structure shows a high concentration of operational logistics costs and increasing marketing expenditures with low ROI indicators.

## Key Findings
- Logistics costs increased by 18% compared to last quarter
- Software subscriptions show redundancy across departments
- Marketing spending is not aligned with revenue growth

## Risk Areas
- Dependency on a single logistics provider
- High fixed SaaS costs

## Optimization Suggestions
- Vendor renegotiation in logistics
- Consolidation of digital tools
- Performance-based marketing budgeting
